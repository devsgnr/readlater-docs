**What Readlater does**

- **Save anything instantly** — highlight a passage and right-click to save, or click the Chrome extension to save an entire page. No decisions required.
- **Find it all in one place** — every saved text lives in a clean, distraction-free library that's easy to navigate.
- **Search by meaning** — Readlater's semantic search understands what you're looking for, even when you can't remember the exact words.
- **Organize into collections** — group your saved texts by topic, keep them private or share them publicly so others can benefit too.
- **Save PDFs** — save research papers and documents (we backup PDFs under 10MB) and Readlater extracts a clean summary for you.

**Take it further with MCP**

Readlater connects to your AI tools via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). This means you can save articles, search your library, and organize collections directly from Claude or Cursor — and your AI gets context from everything you've been reading.
