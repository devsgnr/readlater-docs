**What you can do with the Readlater MCP**

- Save any article or text instantly from a URL
- Search across your entire reading library by meaning, not just keywords
- Add saved texts to collections to keep your knowledge organized

**Where you can use it**

The Readlater MCP works with any MCP-compatible client, including:

- **Claude** (Claude.ai and Claude Desktop)
- **Cursor**

**Get started for Free**

Free users receive 250 MCP credits on signup — enough to start building your reading library and plugging it into your workflow right away.
